# Eular Equation
$$\begin{aligned}
e^{i\theta}&=\cos\theta+i\sin\theta \\
e^{-i\theta}&=\cos\theta-i\sin\theta \\
\cos\theta&=\frac{e^{i\theta}+e^{-i\theta}}{2} \\
\sin\theta&=\frac{ie^{-i\theta}-ie^{i\theta}}{2}
\end{aligned}$$
