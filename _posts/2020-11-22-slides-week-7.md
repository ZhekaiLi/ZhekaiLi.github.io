# L11 Orthogonality
## Normed vector spaces

> ##### Normed vector space
> $X$ is normed if there is a function $\|\cdot\|:X\to\mathbb{R}$, such that
$$ \|x\| \
\|x+y\|\leq\|x\|+\|y\|
$$
